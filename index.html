Life Cycle:   the way living things continuously grow, make new living things, and die.
ALL ABOUT VOCABULARY
Parent:   a living thing that makes a new living thing.
Frog:   Short bodied, amphibian without a tail.
Young:   a living thing that was just recently made by its parent.
Froglets are young frogs.
Frog Spawn:   Frog eggs that are moist and covered with a jelly-like coating.
Tadpole: Swims in the water and breathes using gills.
Froglet: An almost adult frog that has some of its tail and breathes using lungs.
Amphibian: An animal that spends part of its life in water and part on land.
